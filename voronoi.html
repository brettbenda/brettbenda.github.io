Voronoi Diagram Generator

Below are two processing sketches that generate Voronoi diagrams. Every point is colored in the canvas according to which point it is closest to. The two sketches use different rules to determine distance. In both, you can click and drag points around as well as add new points.

Euclidean Distace: distance calculated by only taking a straight-line path

Manhattan Distace: distance calculated by only taking vertical or horizontal paths
