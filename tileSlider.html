Tile Slider

Link to GitHub Repository

Tile Slider is a game I developed with a team as our final project for CAP 3032 Interactive Modeling and Animation.

In Tile Slider, the player manipulates the gameboard to match a pattern. At the beginning of each game, a random gameboard and corresponding pattern are generated.The arrow keys shift all the colors tiles in the given direction, provided the space is not occupied by a black "wall" tile or another colored tile, or the destination would be off the board. The player can also use the Undo (blue) button to undo up to five moves, or the Reset (red) button to fully reset the game board. The player also has the option between three difficulties, with higher difficulties having a larger game board to manipulate.
