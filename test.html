Provenance Segment Visualization

Data Processing

Data from provenance logs are segmented. Small segments (<25s long) are grouped into the preceeding segment. Only "interesting" action types from the logs are regarded in summarizing each segment.

Overview

Each segment is represented by a card, featuring the PID and segment number it represents. A green bar represents the timeline for the entire segment, with a dark green block showing the relative start and end times for the segment. The length of this bar encodes the duration of the segment. Key information about searching, note taking, highlighting, and document moving is included. Bars showing the relative frequency of each action are shown for each segment. A "Total" activity bar shows how much of the total activity occurs in the segment.

Zoom/Filter

Nothing present for this. Doesn't seem like its necessary really at the moment.

Details on Demand

Hovering over the timeline shows precises start, end, and duration times. Incomplete notes/highlights can be hovered to show the entire text. Hovering over each bar displays all the interactions of the type from that segment and is linked to highlight corresponding interactions from other segments and shows their frequency. A list of all actions with frequencies is provided
