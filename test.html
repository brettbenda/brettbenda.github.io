Provenance Segment Visualization

Data Processing

Data from provenance logs are segmented. Small segments (<30s long) are grouped into the preceeding segment. Only "interesting" action types from the logs are regarded in summarizing each segment.

Overview

Each segment is represented by a card, featuring the PID and segment number it represents, start and end times, and total length. The card border encodes the length of the segment (thicker border is longer). A naive textual explanation of actions taken during the segment are generated, prioritizing direct actions such as searching/highlighting/note taking over dragging. Bars showing the relative frequency of each action are shown for each segment. A "Total" activity bar shows how much of the total activity occurs in the segment.

Details on Demand

Hovering over each bar displays all the interactions of the type from that segment and is linked to highlight corresponding interactions from other segments and shows their frequency as well.
