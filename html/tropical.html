Tropical Scene

I worked on this with a teammate as our final project for CAP3220 Introduction to Computer-Aided Modeling.

Our guidelines gave us a lot of creative freedom to develop our scene, with the major requirement being we had to use all the skills/techniques taught during the semester.

My contributions to the project are as follows:

  • Modelling and texturing all plants, rocks, and trees
  • All textures but the rock and palm tree bark were made by hand
  • Modeling and texturing the land (sand) portion of the scene
  • Lighting the scene
