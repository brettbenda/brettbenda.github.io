Boat

For this scene, I recreated a boat owned by my family. The hull of the boat as well as the propellor and wheel were modelled using NURBS, and the rest modeled with polygons. To animate it, I constrained it to a path an animated its position using Maya's graph editor. Below, I have included some higher quality images of the boat in a lit scene.
